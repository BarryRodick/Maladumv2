About Deck Builder

Deckbuilder has been designed specifically for Maladum. I found configuring the event deck to be a little clunky, and for a single-player game, the additional setup time was something I wanted to avoid.

Deckbuilder removes the need for you to set up and configure the event deck, select your cards, set the scenario rules, and voilaâ€”you are good to go!

Maladum is a great game, and I am glad to be able to offer something back to the community.

Features

  • Select Games: Choose from a range of game expansions to include their respective cards in your deck.
  • Select Card Types: Specify the types of cards you want in your deck, such as Environment, Revenant, Novice, and more.
  • Difficulty Levels: Adjust the number of Novice and Veteran cards based on your preferred difficulty setting.
  • Special Rules:
    • Sentry Rules: Enable the inclusion of Sentry cards (e.g., Revenant, Malagaunt) to introduce unique challenges into your deck.
    • Corrupter Rules: Enable Corrupter cards that replace random cards in your deck, adding unpredictability and complexity.
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor your deck generation progress with a visual progress bar.
  • Card Actions: Perform actions on individual cards, such as shuffling them back into the deck or replacing them with unseen cards.
  • In Play Management: Keep track of cards that are currently in play and manage them effectively.
  • Persistent Configuration: Your deck configurations are saved automatically, allowing you to return and continue building without losing your settings.
  • Service Worker Integration: Enjoy a seamless experience with offline capabilities and update notifications.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Click on the "Select Games" button in the header to choose which game expansions you want to include. Selecting a game will load all its associated cards into the deck builder.

After selecting your desired games, use the "Select Card Types" button to specify which types of cards you want in your deck. You can adjust the number of each card type using the provided controls.

Difficulty Levels allow you to adjust the complexity of your deck by setting the number of Novice and Veteran cards. Higher difficulty settings increase the number of Veteran cards, making the deck more challenging.

Sentry Rules: When enabled, Sentry cards such as Revenant and Malagaunt are added to your deck when you apply the correct 'Card Action'.
Corrupter Rules: Enabling Corrupter rules replaces a specified number of random cards in your deck with Corrupter cards, adding unpredictability and complexity to your gameplay.

Once you've selected your games, card types, and configured any special rules, click the "Generate Deck" button. The application will compile your selections into a customized deck, applying any rules you've set. You can then navigate through your deck using the provided controls.

As you navigate through your deck, you can mark cards as "In Play" by clicking the respective button. This allows you to keep track of active cards and manage them effectively within your gameplay.
